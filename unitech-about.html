Our Story

Born in Kolkata.
Built on Service.

Founded in 2006 at 2, Canal Street, Sealdah, Unitech began as a small distribution office focused on currency counting machines for Kolkata's banks and financial institutions. What started with a handful of products and a commitment to after-sales service grew steadily into a comprehensive office automation supplier.

Today, we serve over 5,000 clients across West Bengal — from nationalised banks and private firms to retail chains, jewellery showrooms, and government offices — supplying trusted brands for currency handling, paper shredding, electronic safes, CCTV, and more.

Authorised dealer for leading currency machine brands including MIX, Godrej, Kores, and Glory
In-house trained technicians for on-site AMC and emergency repair services
Dedicated support team reachable six days a week, with WhatsApp response within the hour
Transparent pricing, genuine spare parts, and no hidden service charges

Our Journey

Key Milestones

Two decades of steady growth, one step at a time.

2006
Founded at Sealdah, Kolkata
Unitech opens its first office at 2, Canal Street, Sealdah, distributing currency counting machines to local banks and financial institutions.
2010
Expanded Product Portfolio
Added paper shredders, fake note detectors, and electronic safes to the lineup — becoming a one-stop shop for office automation and security equipment.
2014
Launched AMC Services
Introduced structured Annual Maintenance Contracts with a dedicated in-house technical team — transforming after-sales service into a core business pillar.
2018
1,000+ Active Clients Milestone
