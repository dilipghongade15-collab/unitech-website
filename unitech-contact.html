FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to things our customers ask most often.

Do you offer product demonstrations before purchase?
Yes, absolutely. We encourage customers to visit our Kolkata showroom where you can see all our currency counting machines and office automation equipment in action. You can also call us to schedule a demo at your premises for bulk or institutional purchases.
What warranty do your machines come with?
All our machines come with a 1-year comprehensive on-site warranty. This covers parts and labour for any manufacturing defects. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for extended coverage beyond the warranty period.
Do you provide service outside Kolkata?
Our primary service centre is in Kolkata. For clients in West Bengal and nearby states, we can arrange on-site service visits. For remote locations, machines can be shipped to our service centre. Please call us to discuss your specific location and we'll find the best solution.
Can I get a bulk or dealer pricing?
Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk purchases (banks, NBFCs, government departments) and for dealers/resellers. Please contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a customised quote within 24 hours.
Which currency notes do your machines support?
Our machines support all current Indian Rupee denominations (₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, ₹500). Select models also support multi-currency detection. All machines are updated regularly via firmware to support any new RBI-issued note features.
How long does delivery take after placing an order?
For Kolkata and nearby areas, we typically deliver and install within 1–3 business days. For other locations within West Bengal, delivery takes 3–5 business days. Pan-India shipping is available for select products and usually takes 5–7 business days.
